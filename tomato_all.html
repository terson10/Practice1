Agridera Fresh Market Carrots

Agridera aims to be one of the world's leading companies in the breeding, production, and marketing of carrot seeds. We invest intensively in research and development to provide solutions to growers and consumers in numerous countries around the world. In particular, we breed improved disease-resistant cultivars adapted to different markets globally.

Agridera operates through international and local distributers around the world.
